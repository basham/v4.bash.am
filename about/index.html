About

I’m Chris Basham, an interaction designer and front-end developer living in the quaint town of Bloomington, Indiana. Since mid-2012, I’ve worked for Indiana University, designing and developing various student-related web software.

When not slicing fingers with sticky notes or refactoring repositories, I’m desperately convincing others to play a backed Kickstarter game, fervently listening to an audiobook or podcast, or blissfully running trails in my sandals.
